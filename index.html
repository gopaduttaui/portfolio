01. VISUAL STATEMENT

Formulating designs that move and breathe.

I am Gopa Dutta, a Graphic and Motion Designer based in India, operating globally. My focus is at the intersection of static branding layout and kinetic physics. I build structural brand components and align them with custom interactive motion guidelines.

From high-end jewelry brand identities to tech startup product assets and digital interface motions, my core principle is extreme precision. Every movement graph is custom-calibrated, and every logo vector is constructed with long-term strategy in mind.

05. SERVICE PROTOCOLS

Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding the collaboration structure, typical delivery schedules, and creative transfer options.

What is your standard production timeline?

✦
Timelines range from 2 to 12 weeks depending on scope. Accelerated options (under 3 weeks) are available for urgent launches and carry a velocity premium. Standard brand and motion packages usually complete in 4 to 6 weeks.

Which source files are transferred upon completion?

✦
Upon project sign-off and final investment receipt, you receive fully organized source deliverables including After Effects (.aep) files, illustrator vectors (.ai), raw Figma layouts, and high-fidelity output files (MP4, SVG, Lottie HTML/JSON).

Do you collaborate with agency partners?

✦
Yes. I operate both as a direct consultant for private clients and as a white-labeled motion directress or UI partner for digital studios and branding agencies globally.

How does the Project Planner pricing align with real quotes?

✦
The project estimator calculator provides a realistic starting baseline based on my hourly rate and standard module difficulties. Exact quotes are sent after reviewing specific client assets and detailed scope briefs during initial calls.
